Mushroom Rice

Description

Mushroom rice is a versatile dish featuring rice cooked with or tossed in saut√©ed mushrooms, garlic, and butter, with regional variations adding ingredients like soy sauce, herbs, or spices. It can be a simple and quick side dish or a savory main, with some versions, like Japanese Kinoko Gohan, emphasizing umami flavors from soy sauce and dashi, while others, like Indian mushroom pilau, are aromatic with whole spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
  • 1 can condensed French onion soup
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1/4 cup butter

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine rice, condensed soup, beef broth, mushrooms, and butter in an 8-inch square casserole dish.
  3. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes.
